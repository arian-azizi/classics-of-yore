    • Classics of Yore Presents:

    Welcome to the first ever Classics of Yore feature! For the maiden voyage of this new site, I will be taking you through my favorite game of all time, the 2015 smash hit, and quite possibly the greatest and most important game on the PlayStation 4, Bloodborne.

    A spiritual successor to the breakout Souls series, Bloodborne was perhaps the first genuinely salivating reason to buy Sony's eight generation console, the PlayStation 4. Featuring a gorgeously stylized aesthetic, a ground-breaking approach to story and lore, and impeccable level design, Bloodborne remains in my humble opinion the de facto reason to play on PlayStation 4, and exists as the greatest action RPG on the console. Let's dive right in!

    The setting, and in all honesty real protagonist, of Bloodborne is the city of Yharnam, a sprawling gothic metropolis that wouldn't look out of place in a vampire hunter film. Indeed, much inspiration was likely drawn from Bram Stoker's works on Dracula, and of course its mant subsequent adaptations.
